Our 1st Mission

Sadie Mae

Our first mission. Sadie Mae. Sadie Mae is an 8 month old puppy at CrisisDogsNC (for more information visit https://www.crisisdogsnc.org/). Sadie Mae needs fundraising because she needs to be spayed and chipped. Raising $130 is our goal for Sadie Mae, covering both of those costs

Our mission is to help other animals like her, whether that be with thier medical or shelter needs. You can help us make a difference for animals like Sadie by purchasing our rings or donating directly to Sadie's cause. 100% of the profits made by our rings will go towards animals in need, starting with Sadie Mae.
